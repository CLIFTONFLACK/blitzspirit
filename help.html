Logistics

Delivery

UK: £3.95 tracked, free over £40. Orders dispatch within 48 hours and typically land in 2–4 working days. We've moved parcels through worse.

International: Yes. Tracked worldwide shipping calculated at checkout; duties shown upfront, no doorstep surprises. Mind the Channel.

Change of heart

Returns

30 days, no quibble. Quibbling would be nonsense. Unworn, unwashed, tags on — send it back and we'll refund or exchange, your call.

Wrong size? Exchanges ship free. Faulty print or seam? We replace it, no fuss — and we'll want a word with the printer.

Maintenance

Care

Wash cold, inside out. Hang dry — tumble dryers are for quitters. The distressed prints are meant to age with you; they'll look better at year five than day one, like most things with backbone.

Asked & answered

FAQ

Where are the shirts printed?

Printed in Britain on ethically sourced heavyweight cotton blanks. Built to outlast fashion — and most governments.

Is this a political brand?

No. It's a t-shirt brand with a skull in a bowler hat. Read into that what you like.

When do new designs drop?

New files are declassified every few months. Join the Home Guard for first access — subscribers get 48 hours before anyone else.

Do you restock sold-out colours?

Usually, but slowly — we print in small runs to keep quality up and landfill down. Sign up to be notified the moment a restock lands.

Can I use my BACKBONE10 code on sale items?

Yes. We're defiant, not difficult.

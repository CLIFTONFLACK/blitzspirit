The Attitude

It started on the wings of Spitfires — the RAF roundel, painted over Kent in the summer of 1940. A generation later the mods lifted it onto parkas and scooters and made it the sharpest mark in British street culture.

Ours plays the long game: from a distance, a classic mod target. Up close, every ring is built entirely from the word blitzspirit — repeated like a drumbeat. No extra logo. No signature.
